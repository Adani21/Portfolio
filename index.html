ABOUT ME

My background

I’m a curious and creative student with a growing passion for both frontend and backend development.

Over the past year, I’ve been building modern web projects that combine my eye for design with my interest in coding and problem-solving. I enjoy creating digital experiences that not only look great but also deliver solid functionality and performance.

My journey in tech began with a fascination for design, which soon expanded into frontend development and, more recently, backend technologies.
